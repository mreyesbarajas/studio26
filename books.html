Bluestocking Books

Nestled in the heart of San Diego, the local second-hand bookstore is a haven for book lovers and literary enthusiasts alike. With its charming exterior and cozy interior, it exudes a nostalgic charm that transports visitors to a bygone era of literary treasures and stories waiting to be discovered.


This beloved bookstore is a community gem, cherished by locals who appreciate the magic of browsing through shelves filled with pre-loved books. The scent of aged pages fills the air, inviting visitors to embark on a literary adventure as they peruse the eclectic collection spanning various genres, from classic literature to contemporary bestsellers.


For that reason alone, for the final project of the Front End Web Development (FEWD) I course, I chose Bluestocking Books as the site to give a "digital makeover" to. Retaining a lot of the same schemes and the simplicty of the original site, Bluestocking Books website v2.0 aimed at simply updating and modernizing.
