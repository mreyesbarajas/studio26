About me

Hello! I’m María~


Born and raised in San Diego, California and later formally educated in Long Beach, California, I received my B.A in Fine Arts from Cal State Long Beach in 2020. Primarily interested and focused in the world of illustration and animation at the time, I paid little to no attention to graphic design; I even laughed it off at one point. It wasn’t until the [REDACTED] of 2020 that my interests and attention shifted and eventually led me to where I am today (ain’t fate funny).


Illustrator, animator, and now designer, my work aims to be a happy medium of computer graphics with an illustrative flair. So yes, my favorite Adobe program is in fact Illustrator but be it logos, book covers, or video editing, I can do it all.


When I’m not working on assignments or playing around with Adobe Creative Cloud, I can be found playing my silly little video games, chatting with friends across the globe, learning how to crochet, and out and about practicing Mexican ballet folklorico
