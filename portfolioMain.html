Portfolio

Agua Miel

Agua Miel is a fictional honey business that has made a remarkable name for itself in the bustling market of artisanal honey. Agua Miel takes great pride in producing the purest and most exquisite honey using sustainable and environmentally friendly practices.

The Bread Diaries

The fictional magazine series, "Bread Diaries" is a delightful exploration of bread from around the world. Each volume of this captivating publication delves into a specific bread type or style, offering readers a comprehensive understanding of its history, cultural significance, and mouthwatering recipes.

Playing Card

Inspired by their grace, beauty, and seemingly magical ability to hover in mid-air, I crafted an original design that captures the essence of these magnificent creatures. The hummingbird-themed playing cards showcase intricate illustrations of various hummingbird species, each adorned with brilliant feathers and delicate details.

Bluestocking Books

Nestled in the heart of San Diego, the local second-hand bookstore is a haven for book lovers and literary enthusiasts alike. With its charming exterior and cozy interior, it exudes a nostalgic charm that transports visitors to a bygone era of literary treasures and stories waiting to be discovered.
